# Game Commands

## 1. /spawn
**What it does:** Creates a new NPC entity.
**Options:** `blob` (moves randomly), `runner` (moves in a line).
**Example:** `/spawn blob`

## 2. /upload
**What it does:** Loads a custom entity via JSON.
**Options:** Requires "definitions" with color, radius, and behavior.
**Example:** `/upload {"definitions": {"fast": {"type": "fast", "color": "blue", "radius": 10, "behavior": {"onTick": [{"opcode": "random_walk", "params": {"speed": 200}}]}}}}`
